Fly High

Booking flights is made easier with this online application, which offers separate features for administrators and users. A user-friendly design makes it simple for users to browse, compare, and book flights. It also provides access to booking history and pertinent updates. Administrators, on the other side, use CRUD operations and have specific tools for basic tasks like adding new flights, pilots, and airports. The application gives administrators the tools they need to maintain the system effectively and guarantees a dependable flight booking platform for consumers.
