Internship Learning

Starting in OJT

When I began my internship at Gallium, it initially felt like a cultural shock to me, i was at first out of placed on how should i start and work, i was also at first very self-conscious. The way they demonstrated their work environment was surprisingly calm and relaxed. Everyone there was incredibly friendly and understanding, particularly towards us interns. Throughout my internship, I applied alongside some of my friends who were also interning at Gallium, and we had a lot of fun together during our time there.

Learnings

During my internship, I learned a great deal, especially in my role as a junior developer. The company guided us through their tech stack, which included Docker, Node.js, Blitz, MariaDB, MongoDB, and React. Initially, they tasked us with becoming familiar with Docker and Node.js by providing documentation and examples. Throughout the internship, I gained insights into how a tech company collaborates for client projects. We were granted access to a Jira Board containing various tickets outlining tasks and deadlines. Following completion, we would demo the applications we developed.

Projects

My Works

Farmerce

Farmerce is an web app, that provides an e-commerce website that will help support our local farmers that will solve their problems during the pandemic. Netizens would be able to post and purchase products on our website for easy transaction of the product of their choice. The farmers will be able to use our website to view, create and update the products that they have listed. Only the developers or administrators would be able to delete an existing product.

HartCheck

HartCheck is a mobile and web application. The application consist of users both patient and healthcare professionals, which the patient can connect to their assigned HealthCare Professionals. For the mobile application only the patient has access to its features while the web application the Healthcare Professional has the access to it. The mobile application has many different features that the patient can do, being able to manually put their blood pressure and show a graphical representation, a request for scheduling an appointment a chatting system that they can use to connect to their assigned Doctor. The web application can mostly do all those things as well, but for the scheduling their the ones are able to put the schedule for the patient to book and they can see the blood pressure that they have save in the mobile application.

Fly High

Booking flights is made easier with this online application, which offers separate features for administrators and users. A user-friendly design makes it simple for users to browse, compare, and book flights. It also provides access to booking history and pertinent updates. Administrators, on the other side, use CRUD operations and have specific tools for basic tasks like adding new flights, pilots, and airports. The application gives administrators the tools they need to maintain the system effectively and guarantees a dependable flight booking platform for consumers.
